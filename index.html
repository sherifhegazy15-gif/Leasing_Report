What to do next

Scale the winner

Push budget behind V1

The V1 video delivered June leads at AED 23.69 — 81% of residential volume (600 leads). Headroom to scale spend and test fresh video hooks against the same offer.

Returning demand

90 leads are re-enquiries

90 of 743 leads (12%) are returning enquirers — people who came back after an earlier touch. Repeat intent is the warmest signal in the set; route them straight to a senior closer.

Lift quality

Attack the 55% unworkable

Most leads stall at "Not Answered" or "Irrelevant." Faster first-call SLAs lifted quality for Nisha (53%) & Ruchita (60%) — replicate their call cadence across the desk.

Re-tune retail

Retail CPL is 4× residential

June retail sits at AED 104.68 per lead on just 4 leads. Refresh the creative angle and tighten targeting before adding budget — treat it as a live test, not a scaled line.

Speed to lead

Call the 40 untouched leads

40 paid June leads have zero call attempts logged. Already bought and paid for — working them today is the cheapest pipeline available this month.

Protect the funnel

Watch frequency

Residential frequency is 2.38× and rising. Refresh audiences and creative before fatigue lifts CPM — CPL already creeps on lower-volume days (e.g. Jun 9 & 17).
